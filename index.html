BUILD Package

A package to deliver affordable housing for Detroiters

Boost Investment in Detroit Housing Future Fund

Ordinance 4 would increasing investment in the Detroit Housing Future Fund by $10 million from the General Fund. This will ensure more housing opportunities and development within the city. We hope to gain matching funds from local financial institutions.

Uncover Barriers to Development

Ordinance 5 will create an independent commission to investigate bureaucratic barriers to development. The goal is to identify and address obstacles that hinder growth and improvement.

Introduce Public Program for Construction Insurance

Ordinance 6 will establish a public program dedicated to providing construction insurance for local affordable housing projects. This program aims to support developers and builders by reducing risks associated with construction projects.

Leverage the LA Olympics for Detroit

Ordinance 7 will officially recommend that the LA Olympics relocate some events to Detroit to bolster the local economy and showcase the city's capabilities on a global stage.
