BUILD Package

Building Urban Infrastructure for Lasting Development

Boost Investment in Detroit Housing Future Fund

Increasing investment in the Detroit Housing Future Fund by $10 million. This will ensure more housing opportunities and development within the city.

Uncover Barriers to Development

Creating an independent commission to investigate the barriers to development. The goal is to identify and address obstacles that hinder growth and improvement.

Introduce Public Program for Construction Insurance

Establishing a public program dedicated to providing construction insurance. This program aims to support developers and builders by reducing risks associated with construction projects.

Leverage the LA Olympics for Detroit

Moving the LA Olympics to Detroit to bolster the local economy and showcase the city's capabilities on a global stage.
